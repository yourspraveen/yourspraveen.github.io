# Informed Consent

You are invited to participate in a research study entitled **"Impact of Artificial Intelligence on Project Management Professionals"** under the PMBOK® 8th Edition framework. Please read this form carefully before deciding to participate.

This study is conducted independently by Ragu Ragunathan and Praveen Palaniswamy, project management professionals with an interest in contributing to the body of knowledge on AI and its impact on the profession.

---

**Purpose:**
The purpose of this study is to understand how project management professionals perceive AI's impact on their practices, job content, and skills — across project phases and performance domains defined in PMBOK® 8th Edition.

**Who Can Participate:**
- Currently working or has worked as a project management professional
- Has exposure to or awareness of AI tools in a professional context
- 18 years of age or older

**Procedures:**
If you agree to participate, you will complete an online survey of approximately 20–25 minutes covering your background, AI usage, perceived impacts, and open-ended feedback.

**Voluntary Participation:**
Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without consequence to any current or future professional relationships.

**Risks and Benefits:**
There are no more than minimal risks associated with this study. There is no direct individual benefit; however, findings will contribute to the body of knowledge on AI adoption in project management and inform future practice and training.

**Compensation:**
No compensation will be provided for participation.

**Confidentiality:**
No personally identifiable information will be collected beyond this consent. All responses will be anonymized and stored on password-protected systems accessible only to the researchers. Research records will be retained for three years following study completion, then securely deleted.
