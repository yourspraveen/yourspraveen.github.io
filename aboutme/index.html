    About Me

    Quick Snapshot

    I'm a Senior Manager at Capital One, leading cybersecurity and strategic initiatives. With a Master's in Computer Science and a strong foundation in post-quantum hybrid encryption, I combine deep technical knowledge with hands-on leadership to deliver robust and forward-looking security solutions.

    Professional Journey & Expertise

    I began my career after earning a B.E. in Computer Science & Engineering (Anna University, India), followed by an M.S. in Computer Science (Illinois Institute of Technology, USA). Over the years, I have accumulated substantial experience in:

    • Designing and implementing cryptographic systems — with emphasis on post-quantum hybrid encryption models
    • Steering large-scale cybersecurity initiatives within the financial sector
    • Bridging theory and practice: translating academic cryptographic research into real-world, compliant, enterprise-grade systems
    • Leading cross-functional teams and driving secure software engineering practices

    As a senior member of the engineering community, I bring a mindset grounded in rigorous standards (informed by my IEEE affiliation), strong analytical rigor, and a commitment to long-term architectural integrity.

    Adaptability & forward-thinking — In a world moving toward post-quantum challenges, I stay committed to learning and evolving, blending classical and emerging cryptographic paradigms.

    Clarity & collaboration — Whether communicating complex cryptographic concepts or coordinating with stakeholders across disciplines, I emphasize clarity, alignment, and shared understanding.

    Lifelong learning — My academic journey and professional path reflect a continuous pursuit of knowledge and excellence.
